Why Tennis?

You Will Live Longer

This study shows that tennis not only increases your fitness level but can lead to a higher quality lifestyle and prolong your life by almost 10 years!

You Will Meet Friends

Tennis helps you meet people and stay connected.

You Will Have Fun

There is lots of laughter and fun on tennis courts. Helps you relax and not to take things too seriously.

You Will Feel Empowered

It takes courage to take the racket, walk on the tennis pitch and face your opponents. You can take this skill into real life and apply it in different situations.
